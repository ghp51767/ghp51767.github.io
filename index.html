Georgia Running Backs

by: George Pettway

The University of Georgia's 2017 football team currently has one of the nation's best running backs. His name is Nick Chubb. Nick Chubb is a senior at the University, and he has been a starter on the football team since his freshman year. Not only is he one of the best in the nation, but he is currently chasing one of UGA's most untouchable records: career rushing yards. The record has been held by Herschel Walker since he left the school in 1981. Many running backs since then have attempted to reach this record, but Nick Chubb has gotten the closest to acheiving this feat. Many believe that Nick Chubb will break this record by the end of the season, but I do not think this is possible considering there are only a few games remaining in his career at Georgia.Although he may not be the number one rusher in the unviersity's history, he will always be my favorite running back.
